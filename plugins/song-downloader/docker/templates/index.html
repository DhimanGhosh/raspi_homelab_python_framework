Song Downloader

Download audio tracks via YouTube link or by song/artist/album search. Files are auto-moved into your music library. Place a cookies.txt at /mnt/nas/homelab/runtime/song-downloader/data/cookies.txt to avoid YouTube bot-checks.

Multi song download from JSON

Paste or upload your songs.json from the Windows downloader: {"Song name": {"ytb_link": "…", "file_name": "Song - Album - Artist", "album_art": "…"}}. Songs are downloaded one at a time with a delay between each to avoid YouTube bot-detection — the same sequential approach as the Windows tool.

Retag already downloaded songs

Choose a song from /mnt/nas/media/music and re-apply title / artist / album / lyrics / album-art metadata without re-downloading.

Retag from JSON

Paste or upload your songs.json. Each entry's file_name is matched against existing files in /mnt/nas/media/music and retagged using its ytb_link for precise metadata — the same approach as the Windows downloader. Files not found in the library are skipped with a log entry. Songs are processed one at a time with a configurable delay.
